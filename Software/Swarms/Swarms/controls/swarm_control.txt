Ninefold Swarm Presets:

100: stationary, spread out, slow attraction to preset, stays very close to preset
200: classical bods, orbits around preset when attracted
300: parallel movement in fixed direction, orbits around present when attracted
400: attraction and sudden repulsion, both among agents themselves and presets
500: twirling swarm with lots of movement
600: large independent movements, also large orbits around presets
